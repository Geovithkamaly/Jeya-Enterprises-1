JeyaEnterprises

Established by MR. S. Nicolson in the year 2008, We are one of the leading Manufacturers & Exporters of Dolomite, Calcite Powder Manufacturers in Tuticorin, with a dedicated & skilled team of 1000+ employees. Our vision is to design & manufacture high quality fashion garments for European Brands . This vision is to provide a high quality of dolomite and calcite powder. It has enabled us to develop a long term business relationship with suppliers.

We are constantly adapting and updating ourselves to the latest trends in terms of powder manufacturing technology. All our operations are enabled making ourselves transparent to our Exporters and suppliers on all the information they need regarding the orders and development placed with us.
